60 Hour Shakespeare® rehearses Shakespeare plays in only 60 hours

Hamlet is going to be our 2019 production and will be rehearsed and performed in 60 hours in May 2019.

Sunday 26th May and Bank Holiday Monday 27th May 2018

7.00pm

Caversham Court Gardens, Reading RG4 7AD

Shakespeare and his ensemble might have had only one rehearsal before the first performance of every play. Brevity is the soul of wit.

60 Hour Shakespeare® was created by Gavin Leigh and auditions held at The Scoop, in London, late last year. Much Ado About Nothing is directed by Gavin Leigh and Charlotte Cline.

It’s 1949. In Central Park West, Manhattan, an English socialite has remarried, to a politician named the ‘King’ of the city, following the death of her influential husband. Her son, Hamlet, is in freefall, railing against his mother’s marriage and grieving over his father’s death, when his friends, Horatio and Marcellus, return from Wittenberg University in Ohio.

Get into the sounds and energy with a playlist that captures the chaos and passion of the time.
