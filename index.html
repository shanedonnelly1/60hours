A Midsummer Night's Stream Live via Zoom

We're delighted to announce that although our original outdoor production was postponed due to current circumstances, the show will go on...line!

On Monday 25th May at 7:30BST the May Bank Holiday, 60 Hour Shakespeare's "marvellous cast" is pleased to present A Midsummer Night's Stream, a new production of Shakespeare's classic, rehearsed in only 60 hours, and streamed live from around the world.

Enter a world of dreams, magic and romance as our dynamic cast bring this much-loved comedy to life, online! A feuding fairy King and Queen cross paths with four runaway lovers and a troupe of actors trying to rehearse a play with hilarious, but dark consequences!

We'll be sending the stream details prior to the show, so please register to attend for free and help raise money for young people's mental health with No5 Young People.
